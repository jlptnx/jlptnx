## User Personas

### Primary: The Determined Learner
- **Profile**: Working professional, 25-40 years old
- **JLPT Level**: N2 or N1
- **Pain Point**: Has failed JLPT before or struggles with consistency
- **Goal**: Pass the upcoming JLPT exam (July or December)
- **Behavior**: Uses multiple study tools, needs accountability structure

### Secondary: The Study Group Organizer
- **Profile**: Motivated learner who likes leading
- **JLPT Level**: Any level
- **Pain Point**: Friends keep dropping out of informal study groups
- **Goal**: Create a structured study environment for their group
- **Behavior**: Proactive, checks on others, shares resources
